Project Overview

I. Introduction

Astroboy’s group of six formidable engineers who have successfully found the remedy for precise detection & filtration of ferocious moon dust with the help of data provided by NASA in the NASA space apps challenge 2019.

II. The Problem & Proposal

Lunar dust is proven to have harmful repercussions on astronauts and their space equipment in the past. More specifically, lunar dust can cause astronauts respiratory problems as well as decreasing the life expectancy of hardware. Our solution to the problem is Prophet, a system of devices that detect and monitor the precise location of the dust.

III. The Process & The Technology Behind Prophet

Prophet systems are designed to reduce the risk of dust inhalation, increase solar power production, and extend hardware life expectancy. Each node in the prophet system contains a microcontroller, PM 2.5 sensor, and rechargeable battery that sends data to our central server. The primary server is responsible for processing data and displaying it on our webpage so that the astronauts can stay informed on these issues.
